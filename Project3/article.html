Article 1: Understanding Artificial Intelligence and Its Applications

Last updated: May 20, 2023

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include problem-solving, decision-making, language understanding, and more.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing industries across the globe. From self-driving cars to virtual assistants, AI has rapidly advanced, enhancing efficiency and convenience.
